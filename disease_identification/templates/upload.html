Disease Identification

How the System Works:

Follow these simple steps to identify the disease affecting your crop:

  1. Select your crop from the dropdown menu (e.g., Rice, Pumpkin, or Potato).
  2. Upload an image of the diseased plant leaf using the upload button.
  3. Press the "Identify Disease" button.
  4. The system will analyze the image and display the likely disease affecting your plant.

Ensure that the image is clear and focused for better results!
